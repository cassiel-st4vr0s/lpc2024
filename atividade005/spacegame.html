Space Battle Multiplayer

About the Game

Space battle Multiplayer is an exciting two-player game where you control Space Ships and engage in thrilling combat. Maneuver your tank, shoot projectiles, and outsmart your opponent to claim victory!

Multiplayer Action

Engage in intense 1v1 battles with a friend

Dynamic Gameplay

Rebounding projectiles and strategic space ship movement

Vibrant Graphics

Colorful ships and a space battlefield

How to Play

Player 1: Use WASD keys to move and SPACE to shoot

Player 2: Use Arrow keys to move and ENTER to shoot

The first player to score 5 points wins the game!
